# Ardia — Project Context

## What is Ardia?
Ardia is a finops platform for real estate developers in Mexico. It automates collections (cobranza), provides buyer self-service portals, and manages the full unit lifecycle from available to liquidated.

## Value Proposition
- **For developers:** Stop chasing payments manually. Automate WhatsApp reminders, reconcile in one click, manage units and contracts from a single dashboard.
- **For buyers:** 24/7 portal to see payment calendar, submit proof of payment, and track status.

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
- Real estate developers in Mexico
- Managing 50+ units across one or more projects
- Currently using Excel/WhatsApp/manual processes for collections
- Sales team spending significant time on payment follow-up instead of selling

## Key Pain Points We Solve
1. Hours on phone chasing overdue payments
2. Payment receipts lost in WhatsApp chats
3. No visibility into who paid and who didn't
4. Sales team acting as collection call center

## Product Features
- Automated WhatsApp collection reminders
- Buyer self-service portal (payment calendar, history, receipt upload)
- Unit lifecycle management (disponible → apartada → vendida → liquidada)
- Payment scheme builder (cotizador)
- One-click payment reconciliation
- Facturacion electronica (CFDI invoicing)
- Bank reconciliation via Belvo

## Results
- ~70% less time on collection follow-up
- ~30% more on-time payments
- 24/7 buyer self-service (no more "how much do I owe?" calls)
- Implementation in less than a week
